About us

Scents & Bubbles is a full-service coin-op laundry (washing, drying, and optional folding) service dedicated to consistently providing high customer satisfaction by rendering reliable machines and furnishing a clean, enjoyable atmosphere at a competitive price/value relationship.

Mission

Deliver excellent and affordable quality services that customers can feel relax and entertained through high standard wash and dry machines for laundry shop, friendly customer service, entertaining ambience.

Vision

To be competitive and preferred laundry shop in Roxas Avenue, Bacolod City.

01

Goals and Objectives

• To provide affordable and competitive laundry services. • To make customers feel relax and entertain while inside the shop. • To provide the customers a quality and technologically advanced services. • To reach or exceed customer expectation. • To provide employment opportunities. • To look forward for franchising and expansion. • To be known laundry shop in Roxas. • To offer effective and efficient service to customers.

02

• Provide employees exposure to handle and manage customers and to develop their technical capacity on a day to day operation

05

06

• To prove that propose laundry shop and space rental can benefit people as well as the government. • Comply all the documents and permits required by the government to run the business.

Services

Nowadays, people prefer a high technological machine to simplify household works and save time most especially in doing their laundry.

Self-Service Laundry

The shop offers a highly innovated washing and drying machine which can finish a week-load of laundry in less than an hour. Our business’ goal is to allow customers to wash, dry and fold their clothes, garments and beddings

Self-Service Laundry

for only a short period of time at our laundry shop at an affordable price. The shop will provide an area designated for folding and waiting area.

Additional Services

Aside from offering self-service type, assistance will be offered for beginners. Moreover, for those who don’t have time anymore to do their laundry, we accept full service of wash and dry, fold and press services.

Additional Services

Moreover, a coffee shop is ready to serve while waiting for the laundry. For those who wants to book plane ticket, a travel agency is also available and ready for booking needs.

F.A.Q

We want to make your laundry simple. We want you to focus on more important things and enjoy doing the things you love. We want you to save your time and money on soap, water, heating, and electricity. Our prices are reasonable and affordable.

  • How do I get started?

    Setting up laundry service is easy! Create your account on our website and place your first order!

  • What types of laundry do you clean?

    We clean both every day, personal laundry and laundry for businesses. Everything from teeshirts to tablecloths, we can clean them!

  • Can you clean my comforter?

    Yes! We're able to wet-wash comforters of all sizes.

  • What services do you offer?

    Our basic service is wash & fold. This includes wet-washing garments and drying in a dryer or air-drying. In addition to wash & fold, we also offer: *Fold-only service *Ironing *Dry Cleaning Courier *Bulky Items *Stain-treatment *Deep cleaning (extra wash)

  • What happens if I leave personal items in my pockets?

    We ask that you ensure all items have been removed from your pockets prior to pick-up. In the event that we do find items in your pockets, we’ll return them inside a sealed bag along with your laundry.
